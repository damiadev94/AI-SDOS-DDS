Each phase groups related deliverables. Status badges reflect the current state. Phases are sequential but deliverables within a phase may be worked in parallel.

Notes

A Roadmap Diagram communicates the planned sequence of work to stakeholders. It focuses on "what" and "when" rather than implementation detail. Keep phases broad and deliverables outcome-oriented. Update status badges as work progresses to keep the diagram as a living document.
