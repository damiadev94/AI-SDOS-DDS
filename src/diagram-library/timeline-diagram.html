Date badges use quarter notation. Node variant colour reflects the current status of each event. Planned events use the neutral (default) node style.

Notes

Timeline Diagrams communicate project history and forward-looking plans at a glance. They are most effective when anchored to calendar dates rather than relative offsets. Keep the number of items manageable — if a quarter has many events, consider a separate sprint-level timeline for that period.
