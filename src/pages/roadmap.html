The roadmap spans 3 phases. Phases 1 and 2 are substantially complete. Phase 3 (Delivery) items are pending.

Notes

The DDS roadmap is organized into three sequential phases. Phase 1 established the foundation layer: design tokens, CSS infrastructure, and base components. Phase 2 built the library layer: the diagram framework with 13 types, 4 page templates, and the full diagram library. Phase 3 will complete the delivery layer: the export system (PDF/SVG output), a comprehensive examples page, and a final system review before the v1.0 release. Documentation pages (SPEC-008) are the final item completing Phase 2.
